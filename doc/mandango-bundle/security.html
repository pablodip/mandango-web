Security

The MandangoBundle comes with the MandangoUserProvider provider to integrate your Mandango's documents with the Symfony2 security layer.

services:
    security.provider.mandango:
        class: Mandango\MandangoBundle\Security\MandangoUserProvider
        arguments: [@mandango, Model\User, username]

security:
    providers:
        mandango:
            id: security.provider.mandango

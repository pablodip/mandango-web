Performance

MongoDB is a very fast database, that's why the performance of the utilities we use to work with it is very important to take advantage of that speed.

We've made some test to compare Mandango with DoctrineMongo, another ODM.

The results show that Mandango is 3-4x faster than DoctrineMongo, and Mandango uses 40-60% less of memory.

Benchmark

The benchmark can be downloaded from github: https://github.com/pablodip/php-odm-benchmark
